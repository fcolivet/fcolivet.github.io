About FC Olivet

  • September 2017

    A Humble Beginning

    It was a Sunday morning in 2017. Several passionate players held the first training on the ground beside Dover Immanuel Chapel with dozens of kids, and FC Olivet was born!

  • August 2020

    Almost Heaven, Amenia!

    It was truly amazing grace that we moved to Amenia, the beautiful land like heaven! Boarding school teens, Olivet Academy kids and their parents join in together. Every Sunday afternoon, from 3:00PM to 5:00PM, they are running, laughing and sweating.

  • FC Olivet Has a Dream!

    For Children of God

    FC Olivet dreams that one day, on the green hills of Amenia, children of God from all nations can play together in the symphony of brotherhood. They will be trained to be strong both in spirit and body, to be play-makers that make others great and to lead by serving. And we believe this dream can and will become true!

U12 Team

FC Olivet Kids Team

Kids from 8 to 12 years old usually join our U12 team. We believe that soccer play should be fun for them first, so not much technical training will be provided during this time. However, basic drill, passing and shooting training will be held and they will be able to understand the basic rules for soccer game. They will enjoy the training, match and the fellowship.

Currently, there are 8 players from North America, Europe and Asia in FC Olivet U12 team.

U18 Team

FC Olivet Teens Team

Teens from 13 to 18 years old will be in our U18 team. Compared to U12 team, they are generally stronger and faster. Their training will be more intensive and strict than U12 team. Besides basic techinical training, relatively complex strategy will be taught and discussed among them. They will join High School Soccer League and will play formal matches regularly. They will face challenge both physically and spiritually.

For teens are 13-18 years old, we will also develop programs to do leadership training. Every player will lead warmup, physical training and technique training at least once. We hope that these teens will practice and understand it is to serve to lead, and to be open in their mind and heart that makes other great.

Currently we have 12 U18 players and our Senior coach Jack Wen will lead their training.

Adult Team

Old Young Boys Here!

FC Olivet Adult Team provides an opportunity for parents to play soccer with their children together, for members to do exercises regularly and enjoy fellowship with brothers and sisters in Christ.

Currently there are 8 adults committed to FC Olivet, they come from Europe, Asia and other places. We found that intensive exercise like soccer game actually did good to both body and spirit, it will make people more focused, concentrated and simpler.

We encourage more and more brothers to join us every Sunday afternoon from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM. We firmly believe they will enjoy the 2 hours filled with joy and sweat.
